Transaction Highlights

Infrastructure

$90M Infrastructure Project Financing

Challenge: Asian infrastructure fund needed to navigate complex regulatory approvals and maintain investor confidence throughout a multi-year development project in West Africa.

Solution: Provided comprehensive advisory on regulatory compliance, stakeholder management, and structured a phased financing approach that aligned with project milestones.

Outcome: Successfully closed $90M transaction with government approvals secured 40% faster than comparable projects.
Market Entry

$150M European PE Market Entry

Challenge: European private equity fund required strategic guidance to enter multiple African markets while mitigating cultural, regulatory, and operational risks.

Solution: Conducted comprehensive market assessments, identified and vetted local partners, and structured entry strategy with phased rollout across three countries.

Outcome: Successful market entry with $150M deployed, establishing operational presence in 18 months versus 3-year industry average.
Technology

Cross-Border Tech Acquisition

Challenge: International technology company sought to acquire a fast-growing African fintech startup but faced valuation complexity and regulatory uncertainty.

Solution: Led comprehensive due diligence, structured innovative earn-out provisions, and navigated regulatory approvals across multiple jurisdictions.

Outcome: Transaction closed within 6 months, preserving management team and achieving 2.5x revenue growth in first year post-acquisition.
